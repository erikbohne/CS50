  • Erik Nymo Bohne

    My name is Erik Nymo Bohne and I am from Bergen, Norway. I was born in 2002, which makes me 19 years old. In 2022, I am joining the Norwegian Army to serve for a year.

    I have always been quite an active boy doing sports all my life, but I also have great interest in science. Ecspecially physics and computer science.

  • Sports

    People that know me will say I am quite an active guy. Since I can remember I have had great passion and been eager to play and do different sports.

    Football, gymnastics, handball, ballet, badminton, orienteering, swimming, running and cycling make up the sports I have practised growing up.

  • Science and space

    Ever since first grade I have always had an extra interest of science. In my opinion the interest has emerged from the fact that I have a great understanding of logic, which makes me appretiate science.

    Out of all the subcategories of science, physics is probably my favorite. Being able to understand the logic behind human inventions is quite fascinating. Especially when it covers devices and technology that we use at a daily basis.

  • After my sporting career

    Due to a diagnosis I had 10 weeks of training, which made me think about the future. I ended up changing my life directions and now I train almost just as much, but I do a lot of different activites, such as randonee.

    I am going to the army early 2022, therefor my focus is to work on my overall fitness, including strength and cardio.
